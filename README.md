# MSM Gem Shop
MSM (My Singing Monsters) Gem Shop is a new and intuitive bot that allows you to purchase Gems at a 90% discount, including preloaded accounts with gems.

How to use
------
First thing you will need to do is go to the bot and go to profile and put your MSM Friend Code and Platform, which is required so we can send you your gems correctly. Then, follow these steps:

1. Click shop
2. Click the product type you want to purchase
3. Pick an amount of gems
4. Start the purchase
    * Make sure to confirm your MSM Friend Code & Platform is correct!! You **cannot** get a refund if you put in incorrect information
5. Pay your invoice
6. Wait a few minutes for your gems to be sent to your account
7. You can view the tracking of your order (how many gems you've recieved so far) and your previous orders

### Now, after all of that, you should have your gems!
